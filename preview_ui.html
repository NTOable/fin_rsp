Getting Started

Welcome to IMS Pro! This inventory management system helps you track and manage your stock efficiently.

  • Dashboard: View key metrics and charts at a glance
  • Inventory: Manage all your items with full CRUD operations
  • Analytics: Deep dive into your inventory data
  • Reports: Generate and export various reports

Adding Items

To add a new item to your inventory:

  • Click the "Add Item" button in the top bar or Inventory page
  • Fill in all required fields (marked with *)
  • Click "Add Item" to save

Filtering & Searching

Use the filter bar to find specific items:

  • Search: Type to search by SKU, name, or supplier
  • Category: Filter by product category
  • Status: Filter by stock status (In Stock, Low, Out)
  • Supplier: Filter by supplier name
